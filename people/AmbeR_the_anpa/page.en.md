## Description

AmbeR\_the\_anpa, born on September 1, 2004, from Yichun, Jiangxi Province. Anpa likes to drink at the bar and can also mix some drinks herself. She has made a wish to become the strongest mixologist. Anpa likes sports and has tried table tennis, skiing and other sports. She is also a badminton player in the city team. She plays badminton very well and it is like a shell when she fights. In her spare time, Anpa has also visited many cities across the country.

Anpa was not a trans girl at first, and after two months of dating a trans girl, Anpa decided to transition. Anpa began receiving hormone therapy in 2019. In 2022, she went to Huilongguan Hospital in Beijing to continue treatment. However, when Anpa was in high school, her high school teacher bullied her, which made Anpa suffer from depression and still feel uncomfortable in her daily life. She and her lover chose to work in the country after graduating from high school. Then she went to Weifang to work and adopted a little orange cat. After a while, the domestic environment changed a little. She felt that the domestic environment was not good and Anpa discussed it with her lover. She decided to discuss it with her family and ask them to support her in studying in Japan.

On October 3, 2023, Anpa went to Tokyo, Japan to study while working. During this period, she came to Waseda Street Psychia Clinic to seek a second medical advice, and local doctors supported Anpa’s desire to receive SRS based on the diagnostic and treatment guidelines of Japan’s GID, who was also satisfied with the medical environment in Japan.

Anpa was gentle and considerate throughout her life, rescued stray animals, and participated in voluntary blood donation. She has always been patient and generous with help and support for vulnerable groups.

On July 14, 2024, Anpa attempted suicide and was rescued in the hospital ICU. At 6:12, Japan time on September 17, 2024, Anpa passed away in front of her lover due to life pressure and cyber violence. She was confirmed to have passed away at 11:23 on the same day. After Anpa passed away, her family rushed to Japan and cremated Anpa with Anpa’s lover at the funeral home in Nerima Sakuradai, Tokyo. Since the elderly in Anpa’s family are still alive, they took a cold approach to what Anpa encountered during her lifetime, and everything ended.
